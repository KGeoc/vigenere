Monoalphabetic and Vigenere Ciphertexts
---------------
Each of the following is a snippet of an English-language text, encrypted with either mono alphabetic substitution or Vigenere’s cryptosystem. Pick one, cryptanalyze it, write up how you did so, and find the work from which it is a snippet. This will prove that you understand the relevant cipher. It also satisfies one of the culture tasks.

I will only accept a particular ciphertext from the FIRST person to hand in that ciphertext. If you aren't the first, you will be asked to pick another. I will only accept one solution for each cryptosystem from each person. You are welcome to work together (but still submit solutions individually), but don’t conceal who you have gotten help from or given it to, and make sure you carry your load.
